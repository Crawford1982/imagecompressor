When to choose local alternatives

Choose local tools when images are sensitive, client-restricted, or covered by NDA. Local compression avoids adding a third-party upload processor to your workflow and keeps data residency simple.

For practical use: compress locally first, then upload only to final destination (website/social platform/email).

JPEGmini / ImageOptim alternative notes

JPEGmini alternative: Squoosh with MozJPEG gives strong visual quality/size balance. For quick non-technical flow, use Private Image Compressor at 75–82% quality.

ImageOptim alternative (Windows/Linux): Browser-based local tools are the simplest replacement: Squoosh for deep control, Private Image Compressor for faster day-to-day work.
